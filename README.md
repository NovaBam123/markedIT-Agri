# 🌾 MarkedIT Agri
**Cloud-Sync Field Log System for Precision Agriculture**

> "Precision on the screen, efficiency in the field. From breeding synchronization to automated research logs."

### 🔬 The "Why"
Traditional paper-based logging in the field is prone to damage, loss, and synchronization errors. **MarkedIT Agri** was born to bridge the gap between the mud of the field and the precision of the laboratory. Designed specifically for agronomists and breeders who need high-mobility digital tools that work as hard as they do.

---

## ✨ Key Features
* **Visual Precision Hierarchy:**
    * **Eye-Catching Color Tags:** Designed for rapid identification of different varieties or lines (e.g., distinguishing **Restorer lines** vs **CMS lines**) directly from the dashboard.
    * **Micro-Climate Insights:** Record subtle differences in harvest timing even within the same field, enabling precise harvest estimations and logistical planning.

* **Advanced Export System:**
    * **Global Backup:** Export your entire research database into a single `.json` file for absolute data safety.
    * **Single Note Export:** Download individual findings as standalone `.md` (Markdown) files for reporting or documentation.

* **Field-Ready Engineering:**
    * **Smart Search Engine:** Instant filtering based on titles and content keywords—no more flipping through hundreds of paper pages.
    * **Integrated Agriculture Literature:** Fast-link features to access journals and technical documents directly from your logs.
    * **Cloud-Based Image Logging:** Document plant growth phases (phenology) in real-time with cloud-synced image integration.
    * **Ultra Light-Weight:** Optimized for field performance even on diverse hardware specs (Intel i5-4460 / 8GB RAM tested).
